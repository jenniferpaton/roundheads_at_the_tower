Roundheads at the Tower

Why did Charles I flee the city of London and leave the, heavily armed, Tower of London under Parliamentary control in 1642?

Roundheads at the Tower explores the events that led to the castle becoming a parliamentary stronghold during the English Civil War and the impact it had on the conflict through the stories of people it affected
